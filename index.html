About the NYUAD Library

The NYUAD Library is at the heart of NYU Abu Dhabi's campus. We provide a wide range of academic resources, from books to digital materials, to support your studies and research. Whether you need help with your studies or access to valuable academic content, we're here to assist you. Our library is where learning begins, and we're here to help you excel in your academic journey at NYUAD.
