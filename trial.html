About Us

Welcome to the NYUAD Library! We are a hub of knowledge and resources, serving the academic community at NYU Abu Dhabi. Our mission is to provide access to a diverse range of materials and support research, learning, and intellectual discovery.

Our dedicated team of librarians and staff is here to assist you in your academic journey. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or researcher, we are committed to helping you make the most of our extensive collection and services.

Collections

Discover our vast collection of resources, including:

  • Books and e-books
  • Journals and e-journals
  • Digital databases
  • Specialized research materials

Explore the wealth of knowledge at your fingertips and make use of our state-of-the-art facilities for research and study.

Services

We offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the needs of our diverse community:

  • Research assistance
  • Interlibrary loan
  • Workshops and training
  • Access to rare and special collections

Our goal is to empower you in your academic pursuits and provide the support you need to excel.
